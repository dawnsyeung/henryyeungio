How to Play

  • WASD or arrow keys to move. Aim with the mouse and left-click to fire.
  • Every enemy takedown instantly equips a randomly rolled gun with unique stats.
  • You start with 10 lives. Colliding with an enemy costs one life.
  • Keep killing to climb the leaderboard UI and chase your longest survival streak.

Pro tip: Some guns fire slower but hit harder. Others spray fast but deal less per shot. Adapt fast!
