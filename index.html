Combat Manual

Human squads push in waves. Slice the angle, keep armor intact, and never unlock the visor mid-volley.

  • Lock the cursor before you move — the visor routes mouse input directly into your aim.
  • Hold W to drive forward and close distance before the squad fans out.
  • Feather A/D strafes while firing to stay slippery without losing sightlines.
  • Hold the trigger (mouse, touch, or the Prime control) to auto-fire when the rifle cools.
  • Armor is finite. Use cover blocks to break line-of-sight when volleys stack up.
  • R is an instant redeploy if a wave overran you. Pace waves to keep pressure manageable.
