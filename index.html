How to Play

  • Click anywhere on the arena to lock the pointer and enter the visor.
  • WASD (or arrow keys) move you. Move the mouse to look around, left-click to fire.
  • Every enemy takedown instantly equips a new gun archetype with its own stats.
  • You start with 10 lives. Letting a drone touch you costs one life.
  • Press Esc to release pointer lock anytime.

Pro tip: Spray weapons track fast movers, while slow cannons delete tougher drones. Swap styles on the fly!
