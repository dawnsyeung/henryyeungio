How to Play

Buffered jumps trigger on the first possible frame. Staying grounded refreshes the buffer.

  • Press Space, W, Up Arrow, or tap to jump. Hold to queue a buffer.
  • R resets instantly. P or the Pause button toggles slow-motion inspect mode.
  • Coyote time lets you leave a block for ~0.12s and still jump.
  • The momentum stat shows blocks per second — higher values mean longer gaps.
  • Land in the middle of platforms to avoid clipping short gaps as speed rises.
